Hour of Code

On December 11, at 19:00, a virtual “Hour of Code” event was held by ADA University students at Baku Secondary School No. 271. 25 pupils actively and enthusiastically participated in this event and earned their certificates.

The event was organized by 4 students from Team 11

  • Fuad Jafarov- Video recording, explaining tasks on code.org and presenting slides
  • Nargiz Maharramli- Writing a short report, arranging a school, editing videos & individual introduction to Hour of Code presentation
  • Gulnara Huseynova- Slideshow about ADA University, answering students’ questions and explaining tasks
  • Murad Rajabov- Slideshow about ADA University, answering students’ questions and explaining tasks.

The Process

We started the event by introducing ourselves and presenting slides about ADA University and answered students' questions about our SITE school, financial aid and the process of applying here. After, we showed them slides about Hour of Code and explained the main purpose and goal of this project. Even pupils who said that their dream major is not related to Computer Science, admitted that they were excited to start this activity.

During the event, we explained them how to solve the puzzles and answered their questions. They were actively raising their hands to tell their solutions and ask for help, if needed.

We received a lot of positive feedback from them and we think that our goal to inspire them to start coding and introduce them to the Computer related study fields was accomplished.
